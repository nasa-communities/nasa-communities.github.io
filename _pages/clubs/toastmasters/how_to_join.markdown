## Club 3496, District 36, Area 42

# How to Join Goddard Toastmasters

*Last updated: December 4, 2025*

We invite all of you to join as a member of Goddard Toastmasters and become an accomplished communicator and leader.

## Quick Steps {#quick-steps}

1. **Download and fill out** the membership application form
2. **Calculate** your payment (see [Payment Information](#payment-information) below)
3. **Submit** the form and payment to a club officer
4. **Start attending** meetings and begin your Toastmasters journey!

## Detailed Instructions {#detailed-instructions}

### Step 1: Fill Out the Membership Form

Download the official Toastmasters International Membership Application Form:

- **[Download from Toastmasters International](https://www.toastmasters.org/resources/membership-application)** - Official form
- Or contact our [VP Membership](/clubs/toastmasters/#club-officers) for assistance

**Club Information to Include:**
- **Club Number:** 3496
- **Club Name:** Goddard Toastmasters
- **Area:** 42
- **Division:** D
- **District:** 36

### Step 2: Calculate Your Payment

See the [Payment Information](#payment-information) section below, or contact our [Treasurer](/clubs/toastmasters/#club-officers) for assistance calculating your dues.

For detailed payment instructions and examples, see our [Membership Application Instructions](/clubs/toastmasters/member_app_instructions_v2025_12_04/) page.

### Step 3: Submit Your Application

Submit your completed application form and payment to:

- **[VP Membership](/clubs/toastmasters/#club-officers)** - Primary contact for new members
- **[Treasurer](/clubs/toastmasters/#club-officers)** - For payment questions
- **Any club officer** - [See full list of officers](/clubs/toastmasters/#club-officers)

You can submit:
- **In person** at a club meeting (2nd and 4th Wednesdays, 11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.)
- **Via email** to the VP Membership or Treasurer
- **By contacting** any club officer to arrange submission

## Payment Information {#payment-information}

### Current Fees (2025)

**Toastmasters International Fees:**
- **New Member Fee:** $25.00 (one-time fee, paid only when you first join)
- **Semiannual Dues:** $60.00 per six-month period ($120.00 per year)
  - Dues are prorated based on when you join

**Goddard Toastmasters Club Dues:**
- **Annual Club Dues:** $6.00 per year
- Payable to "Goddard Toastmasters"

### Payment Methods

- **Check:** Make payable to "Goddard Toastmasters"
- **Civil Servants:** Only pay the $6.00 club dues. The GSFC Education Office handles TI dues as an educational expense.
- **Contractors:** Pay full amount. Check with your employer's HR about reimbursement for professional development/training expenses.

### Payment Examples

**Example: Joining in January**
- New Member Fee: $25.00
- TI Dues (Jan-Mar, prorated): $30.00
- TI Dues (Apr-Sep): $60.00
- Club Dues (annual): $6.00
- **Total for Contractor:** $121.00
- **Total for Civil Servant:** $6.00 (TI fees paid by GSFC Education Office)

**Example: Joining in August**
- New Member Fee: $25.00
- TI Dues (Aug-Sep, prorated): $20.00
- TI Dues (Oct-Mar): $60.00
- Club Dues (annual): $6.00
- **Total for Contractor:** $111.00
- **Total for Civil Servant:** $6.00 (TI fees paid by GSFC Education Office)

For more detailed payment information and additional examples, see our [Membership Application Instructions](/clubs/toastmasters/member_app_instructions_v2025_12_04/) page.

## After You Join {#after-you-join}

### What Happens Next?

1. **Application Processing**
   - A club officer will enter your information into the Toastmasters International system
   - You'll receive a welcome email from Toastmasters International with your member number and login credentials

2. **Welcome Materials**
   - You'll receive access to the Pathways learning experience (Toastmasters' educational program)
   - Download your first speech project, "The Icebreaker," from [Toastmasters International](https://www.toastmasters.org/websiteApps/Pathways/IceBreaker/tm100101/resources/8101-ice-breaker.pdf)

3. **Club Integration**
   - VP Membership will add you to the club email list
   - You'll be added to the club meeting schedule
   - A mentor will be assigned to help guide you through your first speeches

4. **Start Your Journey**
   - Attend meetings regularly (2nd and 4th Wednesdays, 11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.)
   - Begin working through your Pathways projects
   - Participate in Table Topics and meeting roles
