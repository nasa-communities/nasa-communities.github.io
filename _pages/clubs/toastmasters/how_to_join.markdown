## Club 3496, District 36, Area 42

# How to Become A Member of Goddard Toastmasters

We invite all of you to join as a member of Goddard Toastmasters and become an accomplished communicator and 
leader. Please follow the following steps to join the club.

1. Fill out the membership form
   - [Blank form from Toastmasters International](https://www.toastmasters.org/memberapp.aspx)
   - *or*
   - [Mark-up copy with Goddard Toastmasters info filled-in](files/400%20Member%20Application_FFE_markup.pdf)
   
   Club No: 3496
   
   Name: Goddard Toastmasters
   
   Area: 42
   
   Division: D
   
   District: 36

2. Use [this detailed reference guide](files/member_app_instructions.html) to 
   calculate the total payment including dues and new member fee, or ask a 
   [club officer](https://gewa-tmp.gsfc.nasa.gov/clubs/toastmasters/).

3. Write a check to Goddard Toastmasters:

   - New Member Fee: $20 (one time, you do not pay again)
   - Toastmasters International Membership Fee: ($72.00 per year -
     prorated at $6 per month.)
     
     For Example: You pay $54 if you join in January and you pay $48.00 if you join in February.
   - Club Fee (annual): $6.00

4. Submit the form and check/money to Esther Woodward (Club President) or
   Abou Traore (Club Treasurer) or any club officer.

   Check club officer contacts at
   [https://gewa-tmp.gsfc.nasa.gov/clubs/toastmasters/](https://gewa-tmp.gsfc.nasa.gov/clubs/toastmasters/)
