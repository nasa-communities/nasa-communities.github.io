<h1 style="text-align: center;">Welcome to Goddard Toastmasters!</h1>


**Club 3496 ¬∑ Area 42 ¬∑ Division D ¬∑ [District 36](https://district36.org/)**

Goddard Toastmasters is open to all Goddard employees who want to improve their communication and leadership skills. The club offers a supportive environment to:

- Speak confidently in public  
- Practice and apply leadership techniques  
- Evaluate speeches effectively  
- Use motivational techniques  
- Develop strong listening skills  

## Meeting Schedule

Meetings are held **virtually** on the **second and fourth Wednesdays** of each month from **11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time**

We occasionally hold **blended meetings** that are both virtual and on-site.  
Please check **Dateline Goddard** (internal to GSFC employees), for the current location or contact our VP Education or VP Membership for details. Guests are always welcome!

## Guests and Center Access

> **Note to guests:** NASA‚Äôs Goddard Space Flight Center has enhanced security policies for on-center access.

- On the day of the visit, a Goddard Toastmasters member will meet you at the front gate, sign you in, and escort you on and off the facility
